About Bored.

Ever feel like you're so bored when you've run out of things to do? Well, that's where this nifty web app comes in!

Look at random activities that you could do to spruce up your day.

Create an account and you can save them for later, and mark them off when you complete them!

Each activity has a theme, a social rank (the number of people that could be involved), accessibility rank (how possible it is to do, 0 being the most possible), and price rank (how much it relatively costs, 0 being free).
